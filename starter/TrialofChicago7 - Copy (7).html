Vaccine Hesitancy

  • Posted on 30th May, 2021

  • By Sohom Niyogi

  • Published in Covid

  • 2 min read

Vaccine Hesitancy is one of the most major obstacles between the world and herd immunity from Corona Virus. This issue roots out from misinformation regarding the existing vaccines. Before that let's see where human rights stand on this issue: Many would argue that it is personal liberty and right of choice that can be exercised during the scenario of vaccination. That in whole can be true but in truth is flawed. Human rights are inherently based on the principles of growth of society through the growth of individuals , which means that the rights of one shouldn't put in jeopardy someone else. This concept stands true for all human rights. In this particular context, we can analyse this well, due to the hesitancy of few -which by fact are a minority- the health integrity of others is being compromised holding them back from global herd immunity. So what can be done about this: The only way to do this is to eliminate misinformation which in the modern world and presence of internet is not possible. The government can however persuade people via legal enforcement and incentives to take the vaccine. This is a critical time for all of us. So I request anyone reading this article to take the vaccine and make others aware so the world can final return to the stage we all have been wishing to see.
